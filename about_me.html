Hellow, I'm Martz!

And I am the "CEO" of The Loopy Lola! That is because I am a very loopy lola - though don't get me wrong; I'm 14.
Crocheting has been a hobby of mine since I was 9 and since then, I have been hoarding using an insane amount of yarn. It was only this year I started making complicated works so you might see that my yarn collection is shrinking but my crochet projects are piling up.
You can check them out in my sample works section ^^
(Fun fact: I injured my wrist last summer because of the amount of things I was crocheting :P)

Sooooooooooo... what is The Loopy Lola?
The Loopy Lola is my small business where I specialize in crocheting bucket hats. You will probably spot me wearing one of them soon. These bucket hats are completely customizable so you get to choose your designs to suit your needs!
Remember, though I specialize in making bucket hats, I am not limited to making them. You may inquire for special requests ^-^
